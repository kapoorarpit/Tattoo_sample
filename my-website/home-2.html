Tattoo Prices

Designing tattoos starts from the wishes of the customer. We prefer to customize a unique tattoo for everyone. You can bring a scetch or other material which can be helpful for designing the piece or come.

THIGH TATTOO
$349

With more women getting more interested in tattoo, thigh tattoos have gotten popular. It’s one of the most common places where ladies get inked. Thigh tattoos just have this general vibe of sexiness and sensuality.

BACK TATTOO
$449

Back is the largest area of canvas on the body. It might be one of the best place for tattoo. Artist can add a good amount of detail with its large canvas. If you’re thinking about getting a back tattoo, you are in good luck.

ARMPIT TATTOO
$579

From super cute strawberries and toad stools, to hyper realistic floral and insect designs, check out the best armpit tattoos giving you inspiration for your next inking. They're actually the latest tattoo trend.

LOWER BACK TATTOO
$249

Tattoos or any form of body art is fun, but the one that tops the list is a lower back tattoo. It is not only the epitome of femininity but also adds to the oomph factor. That is why this location is so popular among the ladies.

ABDOMEN TATTOO
$368

Great numbers of men and women worldwide are now considering having stomach tattoos. There is no one to blame here since these tattoos can really make you sexier. Be sure the stomach part of a person is wide enough.

SHIN TATTOO
$439

Legs are one of the most inked parts of the body, and making shin tattoos is a very common practice. Your ankle bones and shinbones lie just beneath thin layers of skin, making it very painful to be tattooed in these areas.

FOREARM TATTOO
$279

Lower arm tattoos are cool and popular because the forearm provides enough space for creative ideas. In fact, a simple, small design can be just as powerful and meaningful as a bold full forearm sleeve tattoo.

NECK TATTOO
$369

There are tattoos that easily grab people’s attention. One of those is neck tattoos, considered by some as one of the more niche tattoo categories because of the unconventional location.
